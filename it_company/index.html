Important Things You Must Know, Before Starting a Software Company

The software industry includes businesses for development, maintenance and publication of software using different business models, mainly either "license based" or "Cloud-based". This also includes software services, such as training, consulting and data recovery. Starting a software company requires start-up money, programming knowledge, experience in marketing and technical support skills. Software companies operate under a variety of business models, such as charging license fees, charging by transactions etc.
